Educational Background


  • Elementary: St. Augustine International School (Graduated 2015)
  • High School: Christ the King College (Graduated 2018)
  • Senior High School: Christ the King College (Graduated 2020)
  • College: Northwest Samar State University (Graduated 2026)


Take a tour through my time in school, a pivotal period that helped to shape the person I am now. Every encounter that I have had has left a lasting impression, directing me in the direction of personal development and self-discovery.

It was in the sacred corridors of academia that I discovered resilience in addition to knowledge. Every challenge, from figuring out difficult subjects to getting over academic roadblocks, was turned into a chance to improve. I gained the ability to pick myself up after every failure and try again, coming out stronger and more determined than before.

But my identity was fashioned by a rich tapestry of experiences outside of the classroom as much as by the rigorous academic requirements.

The lesson I learned most from my educational experience was probably the importance of tenacity and willpower. I discovered that success frequently resides on the other side of hardship, whether it was staying up late to study for tests or persevering through moments of uncertainty.

I'm appreciative of the people who helped me along the way, the teachers who encouraged me, and the innumerable lessons that have shaped who I am now as I think back on my trip. I take the lessons I've learned and the experiences I've made with me wherever I go, certain that my education has given me the skills I need to face life's obstacles head-on and with fortitude.
